## <sup><img align="left" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/44569252/189434056-b1148ff9-393b-43d1-b362-20d97b64b393.png" width="54" /></sup>Shortcuts



<p align="center"><a href="./apps/shortcuts"><img src={require('../07-apps/01-included-in-onion/assets/shortcuts.webp').default} style={{width: 320}} /></a></p>

:::note <a href="./apps/shortcuts">Shortcuts overview</a>
Onion OS **shortcuts** are impressively versatile and powerful, letting you perform quick in-game actions such as rewinding time, fast-forwarding through slow sections, taking screenshots, boosting volume, loading/saving states, and much more — all without ever leaving the game.
:::


## <sup><img align="left" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/44569252/179301923-635b60fa-22de-4cf3-894a-2f3c34702d64.png" width="54" /></sup>GameSwitcher

<p align="center"><img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/44569252/189434217-72ef0daf-c630-4ec9-b96a-30fd76cb709f.png" width="320" /></p>


:::note <a href="apps/game-switcher">Game Switcher overview</a>
The **GameSwitcher** serves as Onion's central hub for seamless multitasking between games. Simply press <kbd>MENU</kbd> during gameplay to instantly view screenshots of your recent gaming sessions, then use <kbd>LEFT</kbd> and <kbd>RIGHT</kbd> to browse through them. Each thumbnail shows exactly where you left off, allowing you to jump back into any game within seconds — perfect for playing multiple titles without losing your progress or having to navigate through menus.
:::

## <sup><img align="left" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/44569252/179315622-e29e0971-87c8-4855-85e6-fc8de4ccd940.png" width="54" /></sup>Package Manager

<p align="center"><img src={require('./assets/packagemanager.webp').default} style={{width: 320}} /></p>


:::note <a href="apps/package-manager">Package Manager overview</a>
The **Package Manager** puts complete control of your Onion system at your fingertips. Effortlessly browse, install, or remove emulators and applications with just a few button presses — no computer required. Whether you want to add support for new gaming systems or try out community-developed apps, the Package Manager makes customizing your device simple and straightforward.
:::
 


## <sup><img align="left" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/44569252/189439761-e03a9684-ade4-4dcc-8fdd-c006a1663c05.png" width="54" /></sup>Tweaks

<p align="center"><img src={require('./assets/tweaks.webp').default} style={{width: 320}} /></p>


:::note <a href="apps/tweaks">Tweaks overview</a>
**Tweaks** is the backbone of Onion's configuration and personalization! This powerful settings hub lets you fine-tune every aspect of your device — adjust **System Settings** like screen brightness and CPU performance, enable **Network Services** for WiFi connectivity, customize your **Button Shortcuts** for instant access to favorite features, and much more. Make your Miyoo Mini truly yours!
:::



## <sup><img align="left" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/44569252/189498482-2590f31f-cca2-46e9-a316-3af98828446a.png" width="54" /></sup>Search

<p align="center"><img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/44569252/189498639-8e2a43a6-4020-4492-b4b1-6e3f0c0d5fd6.png" width="320" /></p>


:::note <a href="apps/search">Search overview</a>
The **Search** application is an essential tool for managing large game libraries. Instead of scrolling through hundreds of titles, simply type a few characters and instantly locate any game by name or keyword. You can even filter entire game lists on-the-fly to show only titles matching your search term — making it effortless to find that perfect game for your mood, no matter how extensive your collection.
:::


## <sup><img align="left" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/44569252/179304061-647e63ff-5113-4a8a-aaa9-4dfda248d54e.png" width="54" /></sup>Themes

<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/44569252/226450380-2d6ec505-c65d-4b67-bbc5-7104e39cd7ee.gif" width="320" />


:::note <a href="apps/theme-switcher">Theme-switcher overview</a>
Onion loves themes! The **Themes** system lets you completely transform your device's appearance — customize backgrounds, menu icons, console artwork, music, fonts, and more. Best of all, your chosen theme seamlessly integrates across all of Onion's built-in applications for a cohesive visual experience.<br />
**Themes** in Onion come from two sources: <br /> 
- [A vibrant theme repository](https://github.com/OnionUI/Themes/blob/main/README.md) featuring dozens of community-created designs, constantly growing thanks to the passionate Onion community <sup><sub>❤️</sub></sup>
- The built-in <a href="apps/theme-switcher">Theme Switcher</a> app that lets you preview and instantly apply any theme — see how each one looks before committing!
:::


## <sup><img align="left" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/44569252/179302722-7fa82e4d-d72d-4b1e-bb4d-96a2a52aaa62.png" width="54" /></sup>Activity Tracker

The **Activity Tracker** app brings comprehensive playtime statistics to your fingertips: 
- Automatically track exactly how long you've played each game
- View your total gaming time displayed right in the top bar
- Share your gaming achievements with friends by capturing a screenshot (<kbd>MENU</kbd>+<kbd>POWER</kbd>) — images are automatically saved to your `Screenshots` folder for easy access
:::

:::


## <sup><img align="left" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/44569252/179318731-7e262588-cb92-4ea3-9001-2991c4f8ccbe.png" width="54" /></sup>RetroArch

<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/44569252/189438841-f419f936-6376-436e-89b5-78ce1f88494f.png" width="320" />


:::note <a href="apps/retroarch">RetroArch overview</a>
A full-featured **RetroArch** installation running on such a tiny device! This powerful emulation frontend is regularly updated from the official repository, and Onion enhances it further with dedicated drivers created by [Eggs](https://discordapp.com/users/778867980096241715) that deliver exceptional precision and performance. Every core has been meticulously configured and optimized, complete with [custom overlays and filters](apps/retroarch#customized-overlays-and-filters) specifically designed for the Miyoo Mini's unique screen — ensuring your retro games look and play their absolute best.
:::

## <sup><img src={require('./assets/optimized.png').default} style={{width: 54}} /></sup>Optimized emulators

<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/44569252/189438841-f419f936-6376-436e-89b5-78ce1f88494f.png" width="320" />


:::note <a href="emulators">Optimized emulators</a>
Onion includes its own suite of finely-tuned emulators, featuring extensive optimizations to **RetroArch** cores plus several high-performance standalone emulators:
- **PCSX Standalone** — PlayStation emulation with remarkable performance that pushes the hardware to its limits
- **ScummVM Standalone** — Point-and-click adventures running smoother than ever
- **Gambatte Core** (GB/GBC) — Features a [uniquely organized color palette system](https://github.com/schmurtzm/gambatte-libretro/tree/advanced-color-organisation) for authentic retro visuals

...and many more optimized emulators to explore!
:::


## <sup><img src={require('./assets/icon-glo-menu.png').default} style={{width: 54}} /></sup>Game List Options (GLO Menu)

<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/44569252/226447388-8c9c95f1-536a-4946-8b0f-f4b8ead0e97e.png" width="320" />

:::note <a href="apps/game-list-options">Game List Options overview</a>
**Game List Options** (GLO Menu) is your command center for managing individual games or entire collections. Press <kbd>Y</kbd> while browsing any game list to access powerful options: select which **RetroArch** core to use, download cover art directly from online databases (WiFi/MMP required), launch multiplayer Netplay sessions, reset save states to start fresh, filter your library with keywords, and more.<br />Thanks to GLO Menu's flexible architecture, advanced users can even integrate their own custom scripts for unlimited possibilities!
:::

## <sup><img title="Connected Features" src={require('./assets/wifi.png').default} style={{width: 48}} /></sup>Connected Features

<img title="Included apps" src={require('./assets/network-features.gif').default} style={{width: 320}} />

:::note <a href="network-features">Network Features</a>
For the Miyoo Mini Plus (equipped with WiFi), Onion unlocks a world of connected possibilities:<br />
- [Multiplayer/Netplay](multiplayer) — Play your favorite retro games with friends over the internet or local network
- [Easy Netplay](multiplayer/easynetplay) — Simplified multiplayer setup that works anywhere (even supports GB/GBC Pokémon trading!)
- [OTA (Over The Air) Updates](apps/ota-update) — Update Onion directly from your device without needing a computer
- [Automatic Time Synchronization](apps/tweaks#set-automatically-via-the-internet) — Keep your clock accurate via NTP through the Tweaks app
- [RetroAchievements](https://www.retroarch.com/?page=achievements) — Earn achievements and track your progress across retro games
- [Scraper](/docs/apps/scraper) — Download game cover art and metadata directly to your device
- [Network Services](network-features) — Enable powerful features like [Samba/SMB file sharing](network/samba), [HTTP server](network/http), [SSH](network/ssh), [FTP](network/ftp), [Telnet](network/telnet), [Hotspot](network/hotspot), [VNC server](network/vnc), and more!
:::




## <sup><img align="left" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/44569252/179305837-59922e34-e18f-45d1-b5fb-89593ee08152.png" width="54" /></sup>Personal files gathered in one place

:::note Personal files 
All your important gaming data is now conveniently organized in a single location: the `/Saves` folder (e.g., `D:/Saves/`). This includes your save files, secondary profile data, configuration files, RetroArch playlists, and play activity database — everything you need to preserve your gaming history. Backing up your entire gaming experience is as simple as copying one folder!
:::
